Our Story

Welcome to Asaivahh Food Truck! At Asaivahh, we believe great food should be both premium and affordable. We specialize in authentic firewood biryani, cooked using traditional methods to give you the best taste and aroma. We started this journey with a simple goal: To serve high-quality food at reasonable prices. Our secret? Handpicked spices, fresh ingredients, and slow-cooked perfection.
